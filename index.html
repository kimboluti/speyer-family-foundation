Speyer Family Foundation

Advancing the Public Good Through Strategic Philanthropy

Who We Are

For over six decades, The Speyer Family Foundation has supported initiatives that strengthen communities and create lasting positive change. Building on our founders' vision, we empower artistic, educational, and social impact activities to further the public good.

Our Mission

Dedicated to advancing the public good through strategic grantmaking and community partnerships, the foundation supports organizations and initiatives that create meaningful, sustainable change.

Our Values

  • Legacy: Honoring our philanthropic tradition while meeting contemporary needs.
  • Impact: Creating lasting change through strategic investments.
  • Partnership: Collaborating with communities and organizations for shared goals.
  • Stewardship: Managing resources responsibly for sustainable giving.
